About Us

Hi! We're a team of students at Canyon Crest Academy in San Diego. On our home page we will be posting a brief description of the top 10 biggest stories of the day. Updated every day before 7PM Pacific time. We will also be posting articles every other week on topics we've researched. The articles will be about what's happening in politics, the economy, and the tech sector. The articles will be 1-2 pages long. Our sources will be listed on the same page. Along with that we'll be writing opinion pieces, each with our own varying political and economics views. Learn more about us down below.

*Disclaimer about articles links*

Due to the fact that our research may contain articles behind paywalls some of the links we list in our sources may not be accessible to those without Wall Street Journal accounts, Washington Post accounts, etc. We will ,however, always post links to other sources discussing the same topic when possible. Sadly this is not always the case due to exclusive article programs enacted by organizations such as the New York Post and the Wall Street Journal.

Thanks for reading! We hope you enjoy your time here!

Sincerely,

The CCA Post Team
